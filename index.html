What is the 50/30/20 rule calculator?
The 50/30/20 rule calculator is a tool that calculates the disposable income which is income after taxes into three categories: wants, needs, and savings.
How to calculate 50/30/20 rule?
To calculate 50/30/20 rule you should use the following formula:
necessities = monthly after taxi ncome * 0.5
wants = monthly after taxi ncome * 0.3
savings = monthly after taxi ncome * 0.2
